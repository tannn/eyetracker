What is Computer Vision Syndrome?

Computer vision syndrome (CVS) is a widespread condition where extended periods of exposure to electronic screens has negative effects on the user's eyes. CVS is a big problem, affecting over 70 million workers worldwide, and millions of children. Recent studies have even found a link connecting CVS and permanent nearsightedness.

Symptoms:

  • Loss of Focus
  • Blurred Vision
  • Double Vision
  • Dry, Red Eyes
  • Eye Irritation
  • Eye Twitching
  • Chronic Headaches
  • Neck Pain
  • Back Pain
